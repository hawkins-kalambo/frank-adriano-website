About Frank Adriano

Frank Adriano, affectionately known as "Bolsheviks," is a Level 2 student pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in International Relations and Diplomacy at Mzuzu University. As co-founder of the Mzuni Hope Initiative, he has demonstrated unwavering commitment to meaningful social impact and community engagement. With prior experience as a class representative, Frank has cultivated strong leadership skills and an ability to bridge diverse voices within the student community.

A passionate advocate for the power of communication and authentic leadership, Frank draws inspiration from Winston Churchill's wisdom: "Of all the talent bestowed upon men, none is so precious as the gift of oratory. He who enjoys it wields power more durable than that of a great king." This philosophy guides his approach to representing student interests with integrity, transparency, and purposeful dialogue.

I am committed to a leadership that ensures service, structure, fairness, and transparency for every FAFASA student.
