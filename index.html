About Frank Adriano

I am committed to a leadership that ensures service, structure, fairness, and transparency for every FAFASA student.

Manifesto – What I Stand For

Introduction & Core Values

Leadership is not just a position — it is service. I believe in structure, fairness and ensuring that every student has a seat at the table. Many students feel unheard, uninformed and uninvolved. This must change.

I promise transparency, accountability, communication and unity for all students.

What I Will Deliver as Speaker

1️⃣ General Assemblies Every Semester

Meetings at least twice per semester for feedback, concerns and accountability.

2️⃣ Departmental Representation

Every department represented officially to close communication gaps and include all voices.

3️⃣ Monthly Expenditure Reports

Accessible online + physical notices. Students deserve to know where the money goes.

4️⃣ Amplifying All Student Voices

No one should feel excluded. I will champion equal representation and inclusion.

My Commitment

  • Fair and impartial leadership.
  • Transparency and accountability always.
  • Consistent updates and communication.
  • All student voices included in decisions.

Conclusion

This campaign is not only about me — it’s about us and our voices. I will build bridges, shape conversations, and stand boldly for unity and progress. Vote for:

FRANK ADRIANO FOR SPEAKER

A Voice You Can Trust. A Speaker For All.
